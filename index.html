Who we are

A tricontinental operator

TerraHit LLC is an American company specializing in the international coordination of trade flows, logistics management and intermediation services between producers and importers.

We act as an international service provider: logistics coordination, customs supervision, and commercial management — ensuring a clear separation between exporting entities, importing entities and the service operator.

Through a network of operational entities in Europe and Algeria, we ensure the continuity of trade flows on the Mediterranean–Europe corridors.

What we do

Our services

01
Logistics coordination

Supervision of maritime and land transport operations, freight forwarder management and cargo tracking on Mediterranean–Europe routes.

02
Commercial documentation

Preparation and verification of commercial invoices, certificates of origin, bills of lading and customs documents according to international standards.

03
International intermediation

Structured matchmaking between producers and European importers. Management of contractual terms, payment conditions and transaction monitoring.

04
Incoterms management

Full command of CFR, DAP and CPT terms. Clear allocation of risks and responsibilities between sellers and buyers according to the operational context.

05
Commercial structure advisory

Support in setting up trilateral models, drafting partnership contracts and optimizing flow structures between entities.

06
Compliance & security

Verification of documentary compliance for export and import. Monitoring of Algerian, French and European regulatory requirements.

Our method

From sourcing to delivery

01

Analysis & qualification

Assessment of commercial feasibility, identification of regulatory and customs constraints, qualification of logistics partners for the relevant corridors.

02

Contractual structuring

Drafting or validation of service contracts, definition of Incoterms, payment conditions and responsibilities of each entity involved in the transaction.

03

Operational coordination

Loading supervision, coordination with freight forwarders, issuance of shipping documents and real-time cargo tracking to the destination port.

04

Customs clearance & delivery

Support through arrival customs clearance procedures, coordination with local teams and delivery confirmation in accordance with contractual terms.

05

Invoicing & closing

Issuance of service invoices, payment reconciliation, full documentary archiving and operational review to optimize future operations.
