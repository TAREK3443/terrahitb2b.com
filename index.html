Who we are

About TerraHit LLC

TerraHit LLC is an international B2B trading and sourcing company specializing in fresh produce and related food products.

We connect suppliers, distributors, wholesalers, and professional buyers across global markets through reliable sourcing, trade coordination, and commercial intermediation services.

Operating from the United States with an international business focus, TerraHit LLC supports cross-border transactions and supply chain coordination for businesses seeking efficient and flexible sourcing solutions.

TerraHit LLC works exclusively in the B2B sector and develops long-term partnerships based on professionalism, responsiveness, and operational reliability.

Our method

From sourcing to delivery

01

Analysis & qualification

Assessment of commercial feasibility, identification of regulatory and customs constraints, qualification of logistics partners for the relevant corridors.

02

Contractual structuring

Drafting or validation of service contracts, definition of Incoterms, payment conditions and responsibilities of each entity involved in the transaction.

03

Operational coordination

Loading supervision, coordination with freight forwarders, issuance of shipping documents and real-time cargo tracking to the destination port.

04

Customs clearance & delivery

Support through arrival customs clearance procedures, coordination with local teams and delivery confirmation in accordance with contractual terms.

05

Invoicing & closing

Issuance of service invoices, payment reconciliation, full documentary archiving and operational review to optimize future operations.
